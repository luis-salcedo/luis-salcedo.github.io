For more information about these third-party ad networks and how to opt-out of their tracking, please visit the Network Advertising Initiative's Opt-Out page or the Digital Advertising Alliance's Opt-Out page.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on our app. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here that it has been updated, so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we use and/or disclose it.
